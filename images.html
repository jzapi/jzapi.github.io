Benefits of Drinking Carbonated Water

Lots of benefits to drinking this bubbly water. A few reasons are:

  • Zero Calories
  • Flavorful
  • Quenches Thirst
  • Can help bring a sparkle to a punch or homemade wine
  • Can be used with baking soda to take a stain out of some fabrics
  • Compliments a meal by simulating a soft drink
  • Helps you burp
